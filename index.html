A Legacy of Elegance in Kalyani

Nestled in the serene township of Kalyani, Hotel Red Velvet has been a beacon of warm hospitality and refined luxury since 2010. We blend contemporary comfort with timeless charm to create a home away from home.

From our meticulously appointed rooms to our world-class dining, every facet of your stay is crafted with passion and precision — whether you're here for business or leisure.

Prime location in Kalyani township, Nadia
Multicuisine restaurant & rooftop lounge
State-of-the-art conference & banquet facilities
24/7 concierge, butler & room service
